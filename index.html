Virat Kohli

Virat Kohli is a world-renowned Indian cricketer and former captain of the Indian national team. He was born on November 5, 1988, in Delhi, India. Kohli is widely regarded as one of the greatest batsmen of all time, known for his exceptional talent, aggressive play, and ability to lead his team to victory.

Kohli started playing cricket at a young age and made his international debut for India in 2008. Since then, he has been a consistent performer for the Indian team, scoring numerous centuries and breaking several records. He has won several awards and accolades, including the prestigious Sir Garfield Sobers Trophy for ICC Cricketer of the Year in 2017 and 2018.

Off the field, Kohli is known for his philanthropic work and has supported several causes, including education for underprivileged children and disaster relief efforts. He is also a successful entrepreneur and owns a fitness brand, One8.

Overall, Virat Kohli is not just a great cricketer but also a role model for millions of people around the world.
